7oz Marshmallow cream
5oz Evaporated milk
18oz Chocolate Chips
4.5C Sugar
2 Sticks (8 Tbsp) Butter
1Tsp Vanilla

Put Marshmallow cream, Chocolate, Vanilla, Butter in a large mixing bowl.

Mix milk and sugar.  Bring to a boil and hold for about 15min.  Stir CONSTANTLY to avoid burning your house down.

Pour molten sugar over other ingredients in mixing bowl and mix until consistent.  Transfer to pan to cool.

Cooling in Freezer is okay if you're in a hurry :-)
